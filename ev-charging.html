Wireless EV Charging System ⚡

Overview

Wireless EV charging uses electromagnetic induction to transfer power from a charging pad to a vehicle without cables.

Key Features

  • No physical connection required
  • Safe and efficient charging
  • Smart parking-based charging
  • Future road integration

Technology Used

  • Inductive coils
  • Arduino / Raspberry Pi
  • Power electronics
  • IoT monitoring system
